This project created with a video of me and dad sea kayaking in South Korea.
I first started by editing the video to less than 15 seconds, and I added looping so that video will continue to play.
After that, I created functions drawing ellipses on both darkest and the brightest points of the video.
Then, I created a pixel filter and created a slight, translucent mirror effect with rectangles to accompany to beautiful waves and the sky.
